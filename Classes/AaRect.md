`Function`

New()
-----

### Description

Creates a new AaRect

### Params

-   **Array of [Vector2](../Classes/Vector2.md)** - An array containing
    two Vector2s.\
    The first is the point in the upper left corner of the rect.\
    The second is the lower right corner of the rect
